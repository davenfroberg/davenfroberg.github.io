A Little Bit About Me...

I am a third-year BSc. Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia.

Passionate about inclusive software development, my dream is a career in building software to enhance the lives of all individuals, particularly those with disabilities. I aim to contribute to a digital environment that fosters autonomy and agency for everyone, regardless of their abilities.

Beyond academics, I thrive on meeting new people and continuously improving in everything I do. Learning is a deep-seated passion of mine, and I'm excited to see where my post-secondary studies lead me in my professional career.

When I'm not busy with work or school, I find joy in playing hockey, capturing moments through photography, watching sports, and exploring the great outdoors.
